--- layout: post title: "County of Des Moines, Iowa, Thanks USASearch Program" tags: testimonial ---

The County of Des Moines, Iowa, recently switched to using the USASearch Affiliate Program.

About its ease of set up and the advantages of using USASearch over other possibilities as their search service, Des Moines County IT Department staff shared:

I was able to set-up the search feature in a few minutes and it is working wonderfully for our site.

I used it to replace the search that was originally set-up by the company that designed our site a few years ago, which was no longer working properly for us, and which we did not want to pay them $100/yr to have an ad-free search. I will eventually customize this, but for now the process of creating it and adding the search feature back on our site was super easy.

Thank you and your company for offering this free service to county governments.

Thank you! We appreciate your allowing us to share your positive opinion that USASearch is a good choice for county governments.

The USASearch Team
USASearch@gsa.gov
